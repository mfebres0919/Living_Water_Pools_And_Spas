Custom Spas & Hot Tubs

A spa turns a pool into a year-round retreat. Living Water Pools and Spas designs and builds custom spillover spas built right into your pool, raised spas with striking tile walls, and standalone hot tubs — warm, inviting, and engineered to run efficiently.

Every spa is finished to match your pool and patio, with the jets, heating, and automation dialed in so relaxing is as simple as tapping your phone — across Fort Worth and the greater DFW area.

  • Spillover spas and raised walls with custom tile
  • Therapy jets, benches, and precise heating
  • Automation and lighting that match your pool

Why Build With Us

We build spas to the same standard as our commercial work — comfortable, efficient, and built to last.

  • Precision tile and finish work
  • Energy-efficient heating & equipment
  • Seamless integration with your pool
