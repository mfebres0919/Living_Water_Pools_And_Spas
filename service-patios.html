Patios & Pool Decking

The deck is where you actually live around the pool — so it should look as good as the water and hold up to Texas summers. Living Water Pools and Spas installs travertine, paver, and stamped-concrete patios and decking that tie your pool into a complete, weather-ready outdoor living space.

From coping and waterline detail to full patios and pergolas, we build surfaces that stay cool underfoot, drain properly, and last — across Fort Worth and the greater DFW area.

  • Travertine, paver, and stamped-concrete decking
  • Coping, waterline tile, and clean transitions
  • Pergolas, shade structures, and full patios

Why Build With Us

We build decking to the same standard as our commercial work — level, durable, and beautifully finished.

  • Proper base prep and drainage
  • Cool-to-the-touch, slip-aware surfaces
  • Clean, lasting finish work

3 Simple Steps To Your Patio

A clear, straightforward process from first call to finished space — no surprises, no guesswork.

Consultation

Design
